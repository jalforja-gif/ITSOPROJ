IP Application Forms

Patent

Do you have an innovation that’s new, inventive, and can be used in industry? You can protect it through a patent.

Utility Model

Is your innovation new and useful in industry but may not be inventive enough? This can still be protected as a utility model.

Industrial Design

Are you in the field of visual design and looking to protect your work? New or original designs may be protected.

Trademark

If you’re a business, distinguishing your goods or services from others may be a competitive advantage.

Copyright

Protection for your literary, artistic, or scientific work is automatic from the moment you create it.
